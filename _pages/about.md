<h2 id="about-me">About Me</h2>

<p>I am currently a PhD student of the School of Computer Science and Technology in <a href="http://www.nju.edu.cn/">Nanjing University</a> and a member of <a href="https://cs.nju.edu.cn/rl/index.htm">R&L Group</a>, led by Professor <a href="https://cs.nju.edu.cn/gaoyang/index.htm">Yang Gao</a>. I received my Bachelor's degree from <a href="hitsz.edu.cn/">Harbin Institute of Technology, Shenzhen</a> in 2020. My research interest includes <strong>algorithmic game theory</strong> and <strong>reinforcement learning</strong>.<p>
